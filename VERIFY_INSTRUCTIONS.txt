Verification (quick)
====================
Public key file: xxxx_pub.pem
manifest: manifest.json
Signature file: manifest.sig
Expected manifest SHA256: <BUNDLE_HASH>


Steps:
1) Canonicalize manifest:
   jq -S . manifest.json > manifest.json


2) Compute hash and compare to <BUNDLE_HASH>:
   sha256sum manifest_canonical.json


3) Verify signature:
   openssl pkeyutl -verify -in manifest.json -sigfile manifest.sig -pubin -inkey xxxx_pub.pem


Per-file checks (anyone can run):
   sha256sum README_GENESIS.md
   sha256sum LICENSE_BUSHIDAI_NON_WEAPONIZATION.txt
   sha256sum HN-2026-0001.json
   sha256sum HN-2026-0001.csv
   sha256sum ER-2026-0001.json
   sha256sum ER-2026-0001.csv
Compare outputs with values in manifest_canonical.json.
